To play piano with keyboard the black keys are form letter Q to P
First half of the white keys first you hold the left arrow button on your keyboard, then you press a letter from A to J, from F/3 to E/4
Second half of the white keys first you hold the right arrow button on your keyboard, then you press a letter from A to J, from F/4 to E/5
If you hold both left arrow key and right arrow key, then you can play from letter A to L and key ';', from B/3 to D/5
It is preferred to play the notes one by one
